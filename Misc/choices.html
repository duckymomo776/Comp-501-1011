Technology Choices

Education on cyberbullying
Hong and Espelage (2017) suggests that while there is not any consensus to identify and prevent cyberbullying, there are steps everyone can take to give awareness and help prevent cyberbullying. They suggest that information is pivotal to help with the efforts. Everyone should check the websites and tip sheets that give them awareness of the consequences of cyberbullying. They also suggest that parents are key to preventing any form of cyberbullying. Parents of children need to respond instantly if any sort of cyberbullying occurs, this helps prevent more negative impacts such as potential suicide and mental breakdowns. Another aspect this research suggests is that people need to be more open to their medical helper. People tend to be scared or unwilling to open up about their cyberbullying experience and this cascades to negative choices that may harm them or others around them.

2-Factor authentication
Two-factor authentication is a system that requires a user to verify two levels of security, to authenticate a user to protect the security of the user. Game companies allow a user to bind their mobile phone to their account. This is done by inputting your mobile phone number or different email to your game account then, a dynamically generated passcode is sent to the user via text message, or email. Setting up two-factor authentication to your account makes it difficult for a malicious individual to get access to your account as simply obtaining your password will not grant them access to your account. This significantly decreases the chances of getting sensitive information leaked out and further secures your account.
